Project Details

Stableorganizer

Started: August 2025

This Django-Tool is designed to help horse owners and stable managers efficiently manage their stables. It offers features such as horse profiles, health records, feeding schedules, and appointment tracking, all in one user-friendly platform. Till now it is only available in German.

Technologies Used

  • Django
  • Python
  • HTML
  • Tailwind CSS

Key Features

  • Organising stable life
  • Connect with others from your stable
  • Share your horse with others and have all necessary information in one place

Till now it is still work in progress.
