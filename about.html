About Me

Hi, I’m Sarah Theresa Schreiner – passionate about Data Science & Data Engineering with a strong focus on Motorsport Analytics.

I try to combine data-driven insights with the passion for performance, speed, technology and data. I also enjoy building applications with Django to make live easier and therefor enjoy automating workflows that make data more accessible and useful.
