About Me

Data had fascinated me from the moment I first saw a database on my father's screen. And even before that, I was always making statistics for quiz shows at just six or seven years old. So when I eventually learnt about the existence of Data Science & Data Engineering and what it does in the summer of 2025, there was now going back. That was when I decided to put my heart into it, fell in love with what data is capable of.

The first time I really got in touch with the capabilities of data, was in Motorsport. I started watching it in 2024 and soon I would follow live timings and would compare laptimes, to figure the best moments for pitstops. There was no doubt that I would try to combine working with data with motorsport analytics.

But I don't only do Data Science/Engineering. I also did a bit of Django and Web Development and learnd Java and object-oriented programming in School. I try to make a place for Web Dev in my life, but my focus is on Data.
